# -FUTURE_DS_01
Business Sales Performance Analytics
Task: Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling
products, high-value categories, and regional performance.
Key Insights and outcomes:
1.	Sale Trend and Discounts Trend over 2014-2017
•	Sales went higher in 2016 ($9K) and then dropped in 2017 ($6K).
•	Discounts increased in 2017 even though sales dropped.
2.	Top Performing Sub-Categories
•	Accessories and Machines generated the most highest sales.
•	Suppliers has the lowest performing sales.
3.	Segment Performance by Category 
•	Consumer segments spends more especially on Technology.
•	Home office spends less on the each category.
4.	Regional Sales Performance
•	The South region is the top-performing region with the sales of $10k.
•	Central region is not performing well with sales accumulating $1k.
5.	Profit Contribution by Category
•	Technology contributes about 59% of total profit which is largest share compared to the other categories(Office supplies and Furniture).

Tools: Power BI
Skills Gained: Business analytics, data cleaning, KPI analysis, insight
generation
Deliverable: A client-ready dashboard or analysis report with clear insights
and actionable recommendations.
